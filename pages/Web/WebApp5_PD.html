E-Voting System Using BlockChain

Project Information

  • Category: Blockchain-based Electronic Voting System
  • Client: University Of Karachi (Department of computer Science)
  • Project Date: 9 March, 2025
  • Project URL: Click Here

The Blockchain-based Electronic Voting System is designed to address the persistent challenges of traditional voting methods by leveraging blockchain technology. The platform ensures a secure, transparent, and efficient electoral process, reducing risks such as voter fraud, ballot manipulation, and inefficiencies.
The system incorporates decentralized ledger technology to safeguard votes, ensuring transparency and trustworthiness. It introduces a dual-role functionality, allowing voters to access election details, view candidates, and track their participation history, while administrators manage candidate lists, voter records, and election results.

Categories:
  • Enhanced Security: The use of blockchain ensures tamper-proof voting records, reducing risks of fraud and manipulation.
  • Transparency & Trust: Every vote is recorded on a public ledger, providing verifiable election results that build voter confidence.
  • Efficiency & Accessibility: Digital voting speeds up ballot counting, minimizes errors, and ensures inclusivity for voters with disabilities.
  • Immutable Record Keeping: Transactions stored on the blockchain cannot be altered, ensuring permanent and accurate electoral data.

This project aims to revolutionize the electoral process by integrating blockchain technology, eliminating long-standing voting issues, and promoting a fair and democratic election system. By addressing security vulnerabilities, inefficiencies, and trust concerns, the Blockchain-based Electronic Voting System paves the way for a new era of secure and transparent voting.
